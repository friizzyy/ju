The Architect

Building the Future.

I design and build websites for startups and brands that actually care about how their product looks and feels. If you want something that stands out and not another template with a logo swap, we should talk.

Philosophy

Most websites look the same because most people build them the same way. I don't start with a template. I start with what the product actually needs to do, and I build from there. The best work comes from knowing when to keep it simple and when to push it further.

I handle both the design and the code, which means nothing gets lost in translation. What I mock up is what gets built. I care about the details that most people skip: the way things move, how fast they load, how they feel to actually use.

Core Competencies

What I Bring.

Full-Stack Architecture

I build the whole thing. Database, backend, frontend. Next.js, React, Node, Python. I pick whatever makes the most sense for the project, not whatever's trending.

UI/UX Engineering

I make things that look good and actually make sense to use. Animations, interactions, layouts, all the stuff that makes a site feel polished instead of thrown together.

AI Integration

I build AI into products where it actually helps. Search that understands context, content generation, smart recommendations. Not AI for the sake of a buzzword.

Performance Optimization

Nobody waits around for a slow site. I make sure everything loads fast, runs smooth, and doesn't choke on real-world traffic.

Data Visualization

Dashboards, charts, real-time data. I turn numbers into something people can actually read and act on. Custom-built, not off-the-shelf widgets.

Scalable Infrastructure

I set up the backend so it doesn't fall apart when you start growing. Proper deployments, cloud hosting, and the kind of setup that just works.
